Why Donate?

Your donations help us pay for parts, registration, and travel FTC competitions. Every cent is used effectively for the betterment of our team as whole and is never wasted on needless things. The closer we are to fulfilling our wish list to closer we are to succeeding and building the best team and robot possible.

How can you donate?

You can donate using a check donation by mail and later on a direct payment link will be available. Please look at Donation Levels to determine how much you wish to donate. Also if you are mailing a check please include a return address and a slip with an email where we can contact you for more information.

Check Donations

Make the check out to: TOPA. Please put Robotics Club in the memo line to insure that the check is deposited to our account!
